Beyond the Fishing

Extra Activities &
Experiences

The Alaska Circuit isn't only about fishing. From bear viewing and sea kayaking to glacier hikes and wildlife safaris — the Kenai Peninsula offers a full spectrum of extraordinary experiences.

At The Hub · Cooper Landing

Cooper Landing Activities

🚣
Kenai River Float Trips
Cooper Landing · Spring–Fall

Drift the upper Kenai in a guided raft — a spectacular float through the canyon with towering peaks, wildlife sightings, and emerald glacier-fed water. Perfect for non-anglers in the group.

🏔️
Kenai Mountains Hiking
Cooper Landing · Summer

Cooper Landing sits at the gateway to the Kenai Mountains. Day hikes to alpine lakes, ridge walks with panoramic views, and backcountry trails accessible directly from the lodge.

🐻
Bear Viewing at Kenai NWR
Kenai National Wildlife Refuge

The Kenai National Wildlife Refuge surrounds Cooper Landing. Black bear and moose sightings are routine. Guided bear viewing tours available through the concierge portal.

At The Corridor · Soldotna

Soldotna Activities

🦅
Bald Eagle Watching
Soldotna / Kenai River

The Lower Kenai corridor hosts one of North America's highest concentrations of bald eagles. During salmon season, they congregate by the hundreds along the river banks.

🧘
Active Recovery & Wellness
Keen Eye Property

The Keen Eye property is specifically designed for mid-circuit recovery — gear drying facilities, stretching areas, hot tub access, and guided yoga sessions available on request.

🌊
Kasilof River Add-On
Kasilof · 20 min from Soldotna

The Kasilof River, just south of Soldotna, offers a quieter combat fishing experience for King Salmon — an excellent day-trip add-on during your Soldotna stay.

At The Terminus · Homer

Homer Activities

🦦
Kachemak Bay Sea Kayaking
Kachemak Bay State Park

Paddle through Kachemak Bay State Park — one of Alaska's most pristine marine environments. Sea otters, harbor seals, puffins, and stunning glacier views from water level.

🧊
Glacier Viewing & Tours
Kachemak Bay · Grewingk Glacier

Grewingk Glacier is accessible by water taxi across the bay. Walk on the glacier, hike to the toe, or take a guided glacier trek — an otherworldly experience at the circuit's end.

🐦
Puffin & Wildlife Boat Tours
Homer Spit · Gull Island

Gull Island wildlife tours depart from Homer Spit to view massive colonies of Tufted Puffins, Horned Puffins, kittiwakes, and murres — a uniquely Alaskan wildlife spectacle.
